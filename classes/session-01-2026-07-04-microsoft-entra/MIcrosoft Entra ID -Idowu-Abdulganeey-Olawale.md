## My Takeaways

- Identity is the new security perimeter.
- Conditional Access enables organizations to make intelligent access decisions.
- MFA remains one of the most effective defenses against compromised credentials.
- PIM supports the Principle of Least Privilege by eliminating unnecessary standing administrative access.
- Zero Trust begins with verifying every identity before granting access.
